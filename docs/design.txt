* Service strategy
  - domino.service
    - fetch json/build deck
    - any domino-specific methods
  - game.service
    - main thread, current state
  - player.service
    - all player-specific commands

* AI deliberation flow (Condition => Instruction)
  - 1. Condition: player hand.length === 1 => Instruction: playDomino(player's.hand[0])
  - 2. Condition: player hand.length > 1
      2a. Condition: player hand has matches to lead and can win -> play highest match
      2b. Condition: player hand has matches to lead and cannot win -> play lowest match
      2c. Condition: player hand does not have matches to lead -> play lowest domino
